COVID-19 patients who receive home-based care or have been discharged from hospital should remain in isolation for a minimum of 10 days after symptom onset, plus at least 3 additional days without symptoms. Health workers need to establish a means of communicating with the caregivers of individuals with COVID-19 for the duration of the isolation period.
